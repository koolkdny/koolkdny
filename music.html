Title: Counterpuntal Love, Duet in G major Op.2
musescore
This piece is about a girl who I love dearly, but do not know whether or not she loves me.
Often I toil over whether her feelings mirror mine, yet never have come upon a satisfactory conclusion.
This piece is dedicated to the cuttiest and prettiest person I've ever met.
Unforunately, I do not have a live recording of this piece. Enjoy this synthesized version in the meantime.
